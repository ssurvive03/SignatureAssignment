Services and Resources to Support You and Your Pet

Paws & Hearts partners with local organizations to help provide you resources to take care of your new pet! Whether you need training, medical care, or just another friend in the community Paws & Hearts will provide you with all the help you need! See some of our services below and reach out through our contact page if you don't find what you're looking for!

  1. Transportation & Pick-Up

    • Shelter Shuttle - Saturday 9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m. (Free ride within 10 miles of shelter)
    • Vet Appointment Drop-Off - Weekdays, 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m. (Call a day in advance to schedule.)
    • Emergency Transport - 24/7 animal ambulance service.

  2. Behavior & Training

    • Puppy Basics (Group) - Tuesdays & Thursdays, 6:00 p.m. - 7:30 p.m.
    • Adult Dog Training - 1st and 3rd Saturday of each month, 10:00 a.m. - 1:00 p.m.
    • Behavioral Training - Appointment Only, Monday - Friday, 11:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.

  3. Medical & Wellness Services

    • Vaccination Clinic - 2nd Saturday of each month, 9:00 a.m. - 1:00 p.m.
    • Microchip & ID Tag Event - Last Sunday of each month, 12:00 p.m. - 3:00 p.m.
    • Veterinary Clinics - Open Monday - Friday, 8:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m. (Emergency hours may vary)

  4. Grooming & Home Care

    • Grooming Program - Weekdays, 9:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.
    • Nail Trimming Clinic - 1st Sunday of each month, 11:00 a.m. - 2:00 p.m.
    • In-Home Pet Sitting Referrals - Daily. Hours vary by sitter availability.
