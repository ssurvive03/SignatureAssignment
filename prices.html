Adoption Fees

Adoption fees help to cover the daily care, spay/neuter, vaccines, microchipping, and testing of the pets within our facility. Adoption fees are listed below so you can plan ahead before coming in!

Adoption Fees
Pet Type Adoption Fee Includes
Kittens (under 1 year) $175 Spay/Neuter, vaccines, microchip, FeLV/FIV test, starter supplies
Adult Cats (1-7 years) $130 Spay/Neuter, vaccines, microchip, FeLV/FIV test
Senior Cats (8+ years) $75 Same as adult cats, but includes senior wellness package
Puppies (under 1 year) $275 Spay/neuter, vaccines, microchip, deworming, training guide
Adult Dogs (1-7 years) $225 Spay/neuter, vaccines, microchip, heartworn test
Senior Dogs (8+ years) $150 Same as adult dogs, but includes senior wellness package

Discounts

  • Bonded pair discount: $50 off total adoption fees.
  • Military, veteran, and first responder discount: $25 off total adoption fees.
  • Seniors adopting senior pets: 50% off total adoption fees.

Return & Re-homing Policy

If your adoption unfortunatley does not work out, we require that the pet(s) be returned to Paws & Hearts so we can safely re-home them.

  • Returnes within 30 days: No re-homing fee.
  • Returns after 30 days: $50 re-homing fee to help cover care.
  • Training available before following through with re-home at no cost!
