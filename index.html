Why We're Different

At Paws & Hearts, we prioritize ensuring that each pet is placed with the right family. Each pet in our facility is thoroughly checked, vaccinated, and tested by a behavioralist. We ensure to match each pet to our customers based on behavior, lifestyle, and home experience.

Who We Are

A local, non-profit pet adoption company that is dedicated to ensuring each pet, regardless of adoption, has an enriched atmosphere with lots of attention and care.

We like to partner with local shelters and pet foster homes to bring all at-risk pets into our facility to provide an environment that emulates a home.

Our Pets

We have a good mix of puppies, adult dogs, and senior dogs. We also have kittens, adult cats, and senior cats. Each of our animals are spayed/neutered, microchipped, and up to date on all vaccines!

Visit our Pets page to browse available cats and dogs. You can also stop in to visit us anytime to meet the pets in-person!
